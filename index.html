001 / ABOUT

Sound is the scare.

KVS Productions is a one-person sound design studio built around a simple idea: the picture sells the scare, but the sound is what makes it real. I design horror audio for games, short films, found-footage projects, and analog horror — the static, the room tones, the things you hear right before something goes wrong.

Every pack is built from layered field recordings, modular synthesis, and a lot of deliberately broken signal chains — tape hiss, bit-crushed transients, mic bleed, dying batteries. Clean horror is forgettable. Dirty, physical, analog horror sticks.

I work with indie devs, horror YouTubers, and analog horror creators who need audio that doesn't sound like a stock library — built specifically for the moment it has to land in.

  • OutputJumpscares · Ambient loops · UI & game SFX
  • ForGames · Film · Analog horror · YouTube
  • FormatWAV / 48kHz · Stems on request
  • TurnaroundCustom briefs welcome
